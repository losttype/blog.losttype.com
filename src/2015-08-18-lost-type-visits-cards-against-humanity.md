The kind folks at Cards Against Humanity’s co-working space gave us a tour during our [trip to Chicago](/fieldtripchi). Half the desks are occupied by the _Cards_ team, as everyone affectionately called them.

The remainder of the desks house the work of game and graphic designers, illustrators, developers, and more. I work in a [Vancouver co-working space](http://chloi.io/office) with a number of independent teams, so I particularly enjoyed seeing what the freelancers and small teams were working on.
