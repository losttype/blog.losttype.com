[Danelle Cheney](http://www.danellecheney.com/), one of Lost Type’s most regular contributors, is hosting some of the Lost Type team in her home city of Salt Lake City, Utah!

She’s organised a variety of type and lettering events along with the Lost Type, the Salt Lake Lettering Club (which she also organises), and the SLCC Art Committee.

## Workshops

A more recent addition to these trips are some _very_ reasonably priced workshops—I would be taking this if I could.

### Points on Point: Intro to Vectorizing Letters Workshop

Professional type designer, lettering artist, and Lost Type co-founder Riley Cran will condense years of experience into a 3 hour workshop that will teach you the true best practices of vector drawing:

<iframe src="//eventbrite.com/tickets-external?eid=30947991275&amp;ref=etckt" frameborder="0" height="400" width="100%" vspace="0" hspace="0" scrolling="auto" allowtransparency="true" class="embed embed-eventbrite"></iframe>

### Design with No Time Workshop

What do you do when a project lands on your desk and the timeline is measured in minutes rather than days? Dan “Grayhood” Gneiding—you know his work as designer and illustrator at Urban Outfitters, Anthropologie, and (of course) Lost Type—will  help prepare you for these inevitable situations, and show you ways of using pattern, colour and materials for rapid image making.

<iframe src="//eventbrite.com/tickets-external?eid=30947932098&amp;ref=etckt" frameborder="0" height="400" width="100%" vspace="0" hspace="0" scrolling="auto" allowtransparency="true" class="embed embed-eventbrite"></iframe>
