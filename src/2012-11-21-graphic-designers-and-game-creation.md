The field of [graphic design][1] is extremely permissive and at the same time fun, versatile and challenging. It can also prove to be quite rewarding, and these are a few of the main reasons why more and more high school graduates are seriously considering getting a major in the field &ndash; or at least self-teach some techniques to get started. Online marketing and print media, the design of websites, and game development are a few of project categories graphic designers have open doors for. Speaking of game developers, graphics designers play a significant role in making a game look and feel good, and casino games do not fall short of graphic design needs either. 

## The Field Of Designing Online Games &nbsp;

In case you have played at least an online or offline computer game so farm you must be aware of the amazing graphics that the advent of specific graphic tools and apps have made possible throughout the years. But even games that apparently seem simple to design require hard work, perspiration and a great deal of attention to details and creativity. For instance, you can [check out the Deal or No Deal game][2] on the games.ladbrokes.com page and see the way the thrills of the beloved and highly popular TV show have been transferred to the computer monitor or smartphone/tablet screens. The rules are simple and identical to the ones of the original TV game &ndash; a box needs to be selected and kept unopened until the end of the game &ndash; whenever the banker calls and the player needs to answer and make the decision. The rest of 26 boxes are opened one by one during different game rounds, or the QuickPick option can be used for a faster pace of the game. The colors, designs, graphics, and animations have all been created by professional graphics designers and game developers, making the game of Deal or No Deal ideal for online play. The stake selector enables fats stake choosing, and the payouts are greater the more the player resist the temptation of opening his special box. &nbsp;
